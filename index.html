Biography

Welcome to my official website. I am an electronic music producer in Kerala. With influences ranging from DnB, Slap House , HyperTechno & Big Room, my sound is characterized by immersive atmospheres, intricate rhythms, and emotive melodies.

Since beginning my journey in 2023, I've released music on my own label as 6528 RCRDS . My production approach combines analog hardware with digital processing, creating a unique sonic signature that bridges the gap between classic electronic sounds and cutting-edge production techniques.

Whether I'm crafting dance floor anthems or experimental soundscapes, my goal remains the same: to create emotional connections through sound and push the boundaries of electronic music.

When not in the studio or performing, I'm constantly seeking new inspirations and collaborating with other artists across various disciplines to expand my creative horizons.
